Veg Pulao

Veg Pulao is a fragrant and flavorful one-pot rice dish made with mixed vegetables, aromatic spices, and herbs. It's a popular Indian dish, often served with raita or a side salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Basmati rice
  • 2 tbsp Ghee or oil
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced or chopped
  • 1-1.5 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, green beans, peas, potatoes)
  • 2-3 tbsp chopped coriander leaves (cilantro)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1-2 bay leaves
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • 2-3 green cardamom pods
  • 2-3 cloves

Steps

  1. Rinse the Basmati rice 2-3 times until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for 20-30 minutes. Drain thoroughly before cooking.
  2. Heat ghee or oil in a deep pan or pot over medium heat. Add the whole spices (cumin seeds, bay leaves, cinnamon stick, cardamom pods, cloves). Sauté for a few seconds until they become fragrant.
  3. Add the sliced onions and sauté until they turn translucent and lightly golden.
  4. Add all the chopped mixed vegetables and sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Gently add the drained Basmati rice to the pan. Mix gently with the vegetables and spices for 1-2 minutes. Add chopped coriander leaves.
  6. Pour in the water and add salt to taste. Stir gently.
  7. Bring the mixture to a rolling boil. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the pan tightly with a lid, and let it cook for 15-20 minutes, or until all the water is absorbed and the rice is cooked through.
  8. Once cooked, turn off the heat and let the pulao rest, covered, for 5-10 minutes. Gently fluff the pulao with a fork before serving.
